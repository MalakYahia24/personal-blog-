This photo was taken from Sufian Street in Nablus.

Nablus is one of the largest Palestinian cities in population and its most important location. It is the headquarters of the largest Palestinian universities. Nablus is considered the center of the northern West Bank, in addition to being the capital of the Nablus Governorate, which includes 56 villages and its population is estimated at approximately (388,321) people according to 2017 statistics. It is also known by the names of the Mountain of Fire, Little Damascus, the Nest of Scholars, and the Uncrowned Queen of Palestine.
